Meet Anthony

,

Anthony Valinoti is the owner of DeLuca's Pizzeria Napoletana, AKA DeLuca's Brick Oven Pizza, in beautiful downtown Hot Springs, Arkansas. A native of Brooklyn, NY,  Anthony, after living in several other cities, fell in love with Hot Springs and decided to make it his home. He found that he missed his home-town pizza, though, and opened his restaurant which is his passion. DeLuca was his grandfather's name which explains the origin of the name.



Anthony is a stickler for quality. So be assured, he chooses only the finest ingredients for his specialty, Napoletana pizzas. He uses such items as hand-crushed tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, parmigiana & reggiano cheese, fresh basil and arugula, portabello and shitake mushrooms, extra virgin olive oil, Capocollo, smoked pork shoulder with Italian seasoning, a Busetto Brothers spicy sopressata, and other exceptional ingredients.

Located on historic Park Ave., DeLuca's Brick Oven Pizza is fast becoming a popular restaurant for locals and tourists alike. People say they are made to feel like family when they walk through the door.

Interestingly, there is actually a rule-making board in Naples, Italy, that governs Napoletana pizza authenticity. It was while Anthony was living in Naples that he learned and trained in the art of making authentic Napoletana pizzas from master chefs, and, as a result, the ones he makes in his restaurant for his customers are truly special.

Arrange to have lunch or dinner at DeLuca's Brick Oven Pizza to experience what others are calling the "best pizza in Hot Springs."  The restaurant menu has a great beer and wine selection, as well as, home-made Italian desserts such as cannolis. You can order an espresso, too.

Anthony and his staff look forward to meeting you and being your hosts. The restaurant is closed on Tuesday and Wednesday and open the other days of the week.

The address is 407 Park Ave, Hot Springs, AR 71901. Call them to order take-out. See you there. (Restaurant details are in the side-bar.)
