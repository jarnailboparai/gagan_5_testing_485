In order to complete your application process, it is mandatory for you to confirm your request for registration.

Here’s What You Need To Do Next:

Step 1. Check your email and find an email from “High Traffic Agency” with a
             SUBJECT LINE: [IMPORTANT] - Please Confirm Registration

Step 2. Open that email and click on the “confirm” link to confirm your registration.

IMPORTANT: It may take 2-15 minutes for you to receive this email. Please check your SPAM, and PROMOTIONS folders if you don’t see it in your inbox in the next 15 minutes.

As soon as you confirm your request, you will receive another email with further instructions.
