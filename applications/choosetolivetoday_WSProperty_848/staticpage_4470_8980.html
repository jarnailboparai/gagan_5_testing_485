Mahmaya Island

mamamaai
Maldegem, Belgium





We were 1 week in Hurghada and booked Mahmaya trip for snorkling with the kids (aged 10 and 8).
This turned out to be the best day of our stay!
It takes about 1 hour to get from Hurghada port to the island, but we saw dolphins on the way up. A small family just swimming along. Great treat!!
The view from the boat as you come up to the island is awesome.
We seats on big cushions, there is lots of place for everybody! The bar is nice (with bottles or cans of drinks like Coca Cola, Red bull, bottled water) and cocktails (try them!!). 
Drinks are 1,5euro.
The food is ok (salads, egyptian bread, pasta, fish, chicken,...) in buffet.
The bathrooms were clean.
Snorkling gear was provided.
We also went on a boat for a snorkling session near a reef. The water was VERY clear.
GREAT! we saw plenty of small fish 

also on the beach of mahmya, just 3m in the water you have lots of small fish and sea urchins (so watch out where you put your feet!!!).
We took the later boat (left at about 10.30 in the morning), so we had the sunset going back (boat left at 5.30)
Which gives us nice pictures.
And the dolphins were waiting for us :-) and they had fun! one of them gave us a real spectacle.

For the kids and even for adults a magical day.

We paid 60 euro for the adults, 35 euro for kids.
Boat, food, gear included.
Drinks were not included, neither the tips :-)
