New Marina

Ideatroll
Oslo, Norway


Taking a walk at the marina is a beautiful experience, having the ocean and the boats on one side and the little shops, bars and restaurants on the other. We chose to just walk along the dockside late evening for the scenic views and the full moon, but if you wish to sit down for a meal or a drink in one of the many bars or restaurants, the atmosphere and environment is enchanting.

Walking the marina at night outside the season was a quiet but not in any way lonely experience, but I imagine the high season afternoons to be somewhat different. The area is central, nice and clean, and I felt safe walking there even late at night.

Visited March 2014
