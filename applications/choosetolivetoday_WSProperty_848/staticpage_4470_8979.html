Fun Fly

KasiaCe
Horley, United Kingdom
Senior Reviewer



We went with Fun fly for a snorkelling. It was my first experience, and they made it amazing! Captain Bebo and the rest of the crew made the time on the boat very enjoyable. Music, dance. And also plenty of pictures taken and video by a fantastic photographer. It was amazing to go with them for the first time experience life underwater. Everybody was patient and professional. We got also a lovely fresh lunch, which I have enjoyed. We managed to see dolphins, and that was awesome!! I really had a great time and would recommend to go with Fun Fly for any kind of activities. Thank you for a great time!!

Visited March 2014
