Horse Riding Hurghada

Hibaku
Reviewer
r


We went to Hurghada mainly for diving and we wanted to do something different one of days.
We decided to try some horse riding and we don't regret our choice. It was a GREAT experience to ride in the desert and finish up by paddling in the sea. We were all beginners and a bit anxious but our guide was very patient and gave us lots of tips and insights on riding.
The day started by Charlotte picking us up from our hotel and then showing us around the stables when the horses were being prepared. All horses are very well treated and the equipment was in very good condition.
My only regrets are leaving this for the last day. I would have loved to stay longer and do it a couple more days!
I would definitely recommend Horse riding with Charlotte and Jo to anyone going to Hurghada.

Visited March 2014
