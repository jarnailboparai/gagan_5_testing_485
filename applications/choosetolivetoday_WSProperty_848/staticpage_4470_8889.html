  • “Great Diving Center” 04/01/2014

  • I can truly recommend this diving center after having done 5 dives with them. The owner, Hisham and his brother Mahmut take very good care of you as a guest. They are very sympathetic and also make safety the number one priority.

    I for example had my last dive a year and two months ago. Mahmut did a quick recap on necessary skills and helped setting up the tank/BCD before the first dive which gave us both a secure feeling about the diving. We did 2-3 skills in the water (max 5 mins) and went on the first dive.

    On the first day, we shared a boat with Happy Divers who have a very nice boat. The next day, we went wreck diving (my wish) and they organized a smaller boat just for the 5 of us. (small groups at this time of the year).
    On both boats the food was excellent and better than in most local restaurants. 

    What surprised me was the very good value for money, 40 EUR including two dives, all equipments, drinks and food, as well as a taxi from and to my hotel. Fantastic.

    Also booking the course was easy, just call a couple of days before and you will be fine. Oh and don't be surprised, the location of the diving center is a bit tugged-back in the middle of construction works... but you will just stay there a couple of minutes. Inside, it's tidy clean though.

    Book with them, it is really worth it, you will have a great time diving with them. Professional, excellent value for money, great people and good equipment.

    It might only be a bit cold in March^^
