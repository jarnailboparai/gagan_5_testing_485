On our third day to Hurgada, our guide took us to Jungle Aqua Park. The aqua park is really wonderful. It was the biggest aqua park I've seen till now, and even bigger than the CLEO Aqua Park I went in Sharm ElShaikh. There are many amazing aqua games that are breathtaking. The staff was also very helpful. Another good thing is that the water was very warm, which allowed kids to play in water without problem.

The place itself, the Jungle Aqua Park hotel is wonderful. Rooms and gardens look very pretty and relaxing. If anyone is planning to select hotel for a relaxing trip, add this hotel to the list. I also looked for more information about it and found many photos that I couldn't see from the aqua park area, excellent photos. Review it at booking.com

The only remark is the food. At about 3 PM, we tried to find food in the aqua park, but we found only pizza and fries, which is not suitable for most of families.
