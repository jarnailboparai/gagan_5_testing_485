Best Workouts For Women

The best workouts for woman would be those which promote the proper combination of diet and exercise as a change in lifestyle. Many of the programs and workouts available for women today tend to target one thing or another. If a woman’s goal is to gain muscle then her workout would be different from an individual whose goal is to lose weight.

In terms of overall health, the best workout for women should include a healthy nutritious diet consisting of fresh fruits and vegetables along with cardio at least three times a week and some form of weight training/resistance twice a week. This would be the ideal workout for women as each of these three will help to promote a lifestyle of healthy eating and exercising that will allow you to reach whatever fitness goals you are striving for. Just running on a tread mill for several hours a day may burn off some excess fat however you could make better use of your time by incorporating some resistance training into your routine.

Here are some basic exercises that you can do that will help to work out more than one muscle group at a time.

  • shoulder press
  • dumbbell bench press
  • pull-ups
  • bicep curls
  • squats

Although some of these exercises may sound like they will bulk you up, it is the amount of repetitions and how heavy the weights are that will determine whether you tone the muscle or increase its size. If you are not trying to gain muscle then you should not train to muscle failure. By stopping short of muscle failure you will develop a more slender and lean look while also maintaining your femininity with a bit of muscularity. There is no one training routine that is the absolute winner when it comes to workouts for women. It is important to remember that you have to work out according to what your fitness goals are and the look that you are seeking. What you see another individual doing may not work for you as that person may have different goals than yours.

Remember it doesn’t matter how much weight you may be trying to lose, there are three simple changes that you need to make to facilitate your weight loss. Although the dieting and fitness industry may have you believe differently by trying to convince you to try many of the new fad diets that on the market or the latest pill that will have you “shedding” calories in no time at all. The basic principles of losing weight will always be the most effective way to lose and maintain weight loss. A healthy nutritious diet, cardio training and weight resistance training are three simple steps you need to incorporate into your lifestyle that will have you losing weight before you know it.
