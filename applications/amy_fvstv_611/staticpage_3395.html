How Do You Prevent Freezer Burn?

Oxidation, dehydration and bacterial growth are the three factors that cause freezer burn, all of which need oxygen to occur. Vacuum packing in our Munro food sealer bags and rolls eliminates oxygen and, as a result, no freezer burn. This means your food will taste and looks as good as the day you vacuum sealed it.

Which Side Of The Bag Do I Place Face Down In The Vacuum Sealer?

Place the bag ribbed side down. If the bag is curling (the end of the roll may do this), place the curled side down over the seal bar and the bag can be used either side up.
